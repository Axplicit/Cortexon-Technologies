Legionella Risk Assessments Glasgow

If you’re a landlord or run a business in Glasgow, you legally need a Legionella Risk Assessment to stay compliant and keep tenants or staff safe. We make it simple.

At Certificates 4 Landlords, we carry out HSE-compliant Legionella Risk Assessments across Glasgow and Renfrewshire — fast, affordable, and no unnecessary drama.

What’s Included in Our Legionella Assessment?

  • Full inspection of water systems (tanks, taps, pipes, etc)
  • Risk level analysis (temperature, stagnation points, etc)
  • Clear PDF report with findings & actions
  • Free guidance to stay compliant moving forward
  • Peace of mind — no lab testing unless required

Who Needs a Legionella Risk Assessment?

By law, landlords and employers must manage the risk of Legionnaires’ disease under HSE guidelines. You need a Legionella RA if you:

  • Rent out a residential property or HMO
  • Own commercial premises with water systems
  • Operate a business, salon, gym, or similar
  • Provide staff facilities with showers or sinks

We help landlords, letting agents, office managers, and care providers across Glasgow stay safe and legal.
