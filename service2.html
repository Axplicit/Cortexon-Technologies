Why is an EICR Certificate for landlords is Important?

An EICR Certificate (Electrical Installation Condition Report) is a legal requirement for landlords and a crucial safety measure for any property. It ensures your electrical installations are safe, compliant, and free from potential hazards. Our team provides thorough inspections, checking for:

✔ Electrical faults & deterioration that could pose a risk

✔ Overloaded circuits that may cause power failures or fires

✔ Earthing & bonding issues for system safety

✔ Wiring condition assessments to ensure compliance with current regulations

✔ Recommended repairs & upgrades to keep your property safe

For landlords in Glasgow, an EICR is legally required every 5 years or with a change of tenancy. Our team ensures your property meets all Private Rented Sector (PRS) safety regulations, giving you peace of mind and avoiding compliance issues.
