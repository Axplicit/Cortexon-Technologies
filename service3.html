PAT Testing Glasgow | Fast, Affordable & Certified

Landlord in Scotland require several landllord certificates to rent out their property, PAT Testing is one of them, Commonly carried out by youre electrician PAT Testing cover Portable items left in youre property, such as fridges, freezers, white goods, lamps, tv etc. Once completed a certificate is issued with a schedule of testing for the property that is valid for 1 year.

Who Needs PAT Testing?

PAT testing is essential for:

  • Landlords renting furnished properties
  • HMO landlords (legal requirement)
  • Offices, salons, shops, and workplaces
  • Construction sites or temporary setups
  • Anyone serious about safety & legal cover

What’s Included?

We test all relevant portable appliances (kettles, toasters, heaters, fridges, etc) using fully calibrated equipment. If anything fails, we’ll let you know — no scaremongering, no upselling.

You’ll get:

  • A full PDF PAT certificate
  • A detailed log of each appliance tested
  • Peace of mind for 12 months
